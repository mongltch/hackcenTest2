OUR SERVICES FOR YOU!

We offer a range of services to help your organisation thrive here are just a few of what you can get when you request our help securing your systems. Do you need help with any of these? CONTACT US NOW!

Network Management

Let us help you manage your network for you from configuring, monitoring, and managing the performance of your network.

Network Management
Network Management refers to the concept of configuring, monitoring and managing network performance. This involves using a range of tools and applications to secure and maintain network architecture.

Penetration Testing

Need help knowing how secure your systems really are? Our testers can easily help ensure you're up and ready to go and prevent your systems being attacked.

Penetration Testing
Penetration Testing is the act of stimulating a real world attack on systems to identify present risks and vulnerabilities before attackers can exploit them. This is important to fix the vulernabilities and stop attacks before they happen.

Vulnerability Scanning

We can help you identify and assesses security flaws in your organization's systems, networks, and software and how to deal with easily through our scans.

Vulnerability Scanning
Vulnerability Scanning is the process of using automated tools and applications to detect defects and vulnerabilities in a system. This evaluates your assets for weaknesses that can be exploited so that they can be patched.

Physical Security

Everyone underestimates how important physical security is when it comes to network security which is why we have a team to implement strong security measures in your buildings.

Physical Security
Physical Security involves physical actions to protect organisations assets from hardware to personnel to minimise the impact of cyber attacks. Some of examples include locking servers and computers in different rooms so only authorised personnel can access.

Data Analysis

Our team can help you analyse, transform and clean your data. Leave the hard work to us and we will report all the results and findings of data you need interpreted or collected.

Data Analysis
Data Analysis is the process of analysing, collecting, cleansing, interpreting raw data to then be used to make informed decisions. This helps draw out conclusions about that specific information so businesses can optimise performance by making strategic decisions.

Software development

We assess the security of your systems but we don't stop there. We can build and implement software to aid in securing your systems even further and tailor it just to your needs.

Software Development
Software Development refers to creating, designing and deploying software to meet a user's needs. This involves creating or debugging code to implement and manage programs to build software needed across many systems.
